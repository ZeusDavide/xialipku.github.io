Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student of Computer Vision Lab at ETH Zurich, fortunately working with Prof. Fisher Yu. Before coming to Zurich, I obtained my Master’s and Bachelor’s degree from Peking University and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, respectively. During my master life, I was lucky enough to work with Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Hong Liu. You can also find my pages at ZERO Lab.
